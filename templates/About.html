Chinook

The Chinook Database is a collection of real data collected from an iTunes Library. It is an alternative to the NorthernWind database but consists of details regarding the various artists and the songs that they possess. For the project, we are considering the Track, Album and Artist tables such that given a particular track, our search engine is able to extract the artist who composed the track.
